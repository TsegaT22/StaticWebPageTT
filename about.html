About Us

Lion Cubs Organization is a non-profit organization that helps fund sanctuaries and Wildlife Rehabilitators to rehabilitate lions who have gotten injured or are unable to survive on their own. Our goal is to protect and preserve the lion species until they are no longer on the brink of extinction. Lions are an endangered species and there are currently about 20,000 lions across the globe. In Sub-Saharan Africa, (the area with the highest population of lions) there are about 200-300 lions within that region.
